About Bite Express

Welcome to Bite Express — your go-to spot for discovering delicious cuisines from every corner of India and beyond!

At Bite Express, we aim to make food discovery easy and exciting. Our platform lets customers explore different cuisines like North Indian, South Indian, Chinese, Snacks, and Desserts, all from one place. It’s designed for restaurants that want to bring their menu online in a simple, modern, and user-friendly way.

The purpose of building this website is to make the food dicovery more convinient and easy for the user and make the Restaurant menu reach at the user's finger. We built this website with the help of tools like HTML, CSS and Bootstrap.
